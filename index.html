Instagram Follow Checker

Upload your Instagram exported JSON files. The app will figure out which one is following and which is followers, then show people you follow who don't follow you back. Any unrelated JSON files will be ignored. Each entry has a checkbox to mark it as crossed out.

Head to [Instagram DYI], click create export, customize information, and select Followers and following. Make sure to also choose json format and all time range.
